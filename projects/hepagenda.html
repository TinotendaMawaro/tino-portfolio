Jobliss |Employee Mangement System

  • Tech Stack: NodeJs, TypeScript,MySQL, VueJs,Tailwind, AWS, Serverless, Cognito, Hasura, Docker, Vuetify.
  • URL: Project Link

Employee Management system that helps finding past projects or planning future needs; assign projects, delegate hours, source the right skills and track progress with ease.

JobBliss walks you through building your talent pool by adding your contractors and freelancers, and existing in-progress work.

It Facilitates internal and external team collaboration by easily inviting team leads and managers to access your vetted talent pool, share and manage talent easily, aligning team efforts with total oversight.
